How It Works

Season Competition

  • 1.Season-long competition based on your best 10 out of 18 rounds — drop your worst scores or simply skip a week.
  • 2.Similar to the FedEx Cup format — accumulate points all summer.
  • 3.Wednesday scores count from April 29 – August 26, 2026.
  • 4.Scores are converted to Stableford points; standings are based on cumulative points over the summer.
  • 5.Leaderboard is automatically updated from hole-by-hole scores via Golf Genius.
  • 6.Entry fee: $100 (essentially $10 per round).
  • 7.The top 25% of players get paid at season end.
  • 8.The leaderboard reflects actual scores from Apr 8 and Apr 22. The official competition continues April 29.
